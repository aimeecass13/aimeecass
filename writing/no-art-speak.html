No Art Speak Magazine

No Art Speak is a Sydney-based magazine championing local artists and accessible arts writing. Our first issue publishes in 2026. I co-founded No Art Speak with Pinga Matereke and Ethan Scotney in mid-2025, and serve as the editor of written content.

As editor, I oversee all writing in the magazine and the N.A.S. Extras content, published periodically on the website and social media. I work closely with emerging Sydney authors to develop their work for publication while preserving their voice.

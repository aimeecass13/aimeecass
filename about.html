Aimee is a writer, editor and artist with experience in conversation design and literary publishing. She brings a considered eye to language and image across contexts, from voice interface writing to fiction, poetry, and editorial work.

Aimee is Co-founder and Editor of No Art Speak Magazine and a creative writer at Apple. She is based between Australia and the UK.

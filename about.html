Writer, editor and artist based between Australia and the UK.

At the core of my work is a love of language; how the right word or well-crafted phrase can tell a story, capture a character or conjure a sense of place in an instant. That same instinct for precision carries through everything I make, from conversation design and editorial to photography and hot glass.

My professional writing spans conversation design, localisation, copy, web content and editorial. Clients include Apple, the Watarrka Foundation and artist Marta Ferracin.

I'm the editor of written content at No Art Speak Magazine, a Sydney-based publication championing local artists and accessible arts writing. I hold First Class Honours in English Literature from the University of Sydney, where I also studied Visual Arts, supported by the James Coutts Scholarship and the University of Sydney Honours Scholarship. My own literary work has been published in Voiceworks Magazine and recognised with first place in the 2022 USU Creative Awards.

Alongside writing, I make work in other materials: hot glass, photography, painting and drawing. I grew up near the Blue Mountains in New South Wales, and a lifelong attentiveness to the natural world runs through much of what I make.
